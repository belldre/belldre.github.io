Meet Myles, My Amazing Bengal Cat!

Myles is a magnificent Bengal cat, known for his stunning rosetted coat and playful personality. He's a true
adventurer, always curious and full of energy. Myles is 18 years old, and his birthday was July 12.
Bengals are a unique breed, a hybrid of domestic cats and the Asian leopard cat. This gives them their
distinctive wild appearance and intelligent, active nature. Myles embodies all these wonderful traits!
He brings so much joy and laughter into my life. His playful antics, affectionate purrs, and intelligent gaze
make every day an adventure. He's not just a pet; he's a cherished member of the family.
